Sponsor Proposal Form

Thank you for your interest in sponsoring the Black Engineers Society at the University of Maryland. We have a detailed packet that describes our current programs, sponsor benefits, and levels at which sponsors can donate it.

Click Here to view the Sponsorship proposal

We have provided a short preview showcasing the different Sponsorship packages that are available.

Platinum Package - $5,000 and above

  • Listed as a co-sponsor on all Celebration communication and literature
  • Four (4) tickets to the annual Student Recognition and Alumni Celebration
  • Invitation to pre-Celebration Networking reception
  • Four (4) electronic Celebration Resume Books
  • Recognition plaque to be presented at the Celebration
  • Priority scheduling of activities, including workshops and information sessions
  • Ad in the Celebration program book
  • Corporate Logo and Link on the CMSE University of Maryland BES Homepages

Gold Package - $2,500

  • Listed as a sponsor of the Celebration (i.e. awards, reception, etc.)
  • Three (3) tickets to the Annual Student Recognition and Alumni Celebration
  • Invitation to pre-Celebration Networking Reception
  • Two (2) electronic Celebration Resume Books
  • Recognition plaque to be presented at the Celebration
  • Priority scheduling of activities, including workshops and information sessions
  • Corporate Logo and Link on the CMSE and University of Maryland BES Homepages

Silver Package - $1,000

  • Listed as a sponsor of ONE table at the Celebration
  • Two (2) tickets to the Annual Student Recognition and Alumni Celebration
  • Invitation to pre-Celebration Networking Reception
  • One (1) electronic Celebration Resume Books
  • Corporate logo on the CMSE and University of Maryland BES Homepage
