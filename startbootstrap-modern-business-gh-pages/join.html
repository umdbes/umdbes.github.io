  2. How To Join?

Just show up to our meetings! You can also email us at bes.membership@gmail.com

We have weekly General Body Meetings (GBMs) on Wednesdays at 5:30pm in KEB 1110, BlackHack (coding/tech group) meetings on Tuesdays at 6pm in KEB, and Study Sessions on Sundays from 2pm-6pm in KEB 1110.

Sponsor (company) sessions might occur on varied days during the week. If so, stay on the lookout for an email and on our social media pages for updates.
